Services

360Wraps is the UAE’s leading provider of interior and architectural vinyl wrapping solutions, offering seamless installations for residential, commercial, and corporate environments. From kitchen refinishing and flooring wraps to office branding, elevator skins, furniture laminates, and architectural surface films, our expertise lies in delivering high-performance, aesthetic, and durable transformations with zero mess, zero downtime, and zero demolition.

Whether you’re upgrading an existing space or rebranding a full facility, we use globally certified films and architectural-grade materials engineered for heat resistance, moisture protection, scratch resistance, long-term adhesion, and flawless aesthetics.

Why Vinyl Wrapping?

Architectural vinyl wraps are rapidly becoming the preferred alternative to renovation due to their:

  • Budget-friendly installation (up to 70% cheaper than replacement)
  • Quick turnaround and minimal disruption
  • Durable, heat- and moisture-resistant surface films
  • Eco-friendly renovation with reduced waste
  • Flexible design options including woodgrain, marble, matte, metallic, textured, and fabric finishes
  • Easy maintenance with long-lasting performance

360Wraps uses premium brands like 3M DI-NOC, LG Hausys, Avery Dennison, and FENIX architectural films, ensuring commercial-grade durability for high-traffic areas.
