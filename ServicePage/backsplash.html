Maintenance Guide for Vinyl Backsplash Wraps

To maximize durability:

  • Clean using mild detergents only
  • Avoid abrasive scrubs or steel wool
  • Use soft microfiber cloths
  • Keep direct flame exposure at a safe distance
  • Do not cut or chop directly on the wrapped surface
