360Wraps Premium Vinyl Transformation Solutions

Transforming the look, feel, and performance of your windows has never been easier. At 360Wraps, we provide high-grade vinyl wrap for window frames engineered to withstand the UAE’s climate, elevate modern interiors, and upgrade exteriors without the disruption of replacement. As one of the leading window wrap services Dubai, we specialise in both interior window wrap applications and exterior window vinyl wrap systems that deliver long-term durability, UV stability, and aesthetic refinement.

Reinvent Your Windows Without Replacement

Most property owners assume that faded, outdated, or mismatched window frames need complete replacement. Vinyl wrapping eliminates the need for removal, reconstruction, or downtime. Our window frame wrapping solution seamlessly revitalises aluminium, PVC, stainless steel, and wooden frames using high-performance architectural films that offer:

  • Weather resistance engineered for UAE heat
  • UV protection with fade-proof pigments
  • Sand- and scratch-resistant coatings
  • Non-invasive installation processes
  • A flawless, factory-laminated finish

By encapsulating existing frames with precision-cut film, we achieve a uniform, modern look without noise, debris, or structural intervention. This method works seamlessly alongside our vinyl interior wrapping Dubai services to create a cohesive aesthetic throughout your property.

Exterior Window Vinyl Wrap – Built for UAE Temperatures

Harsh desert conditions, intense sunlight, and fluctuating humidity levels demand a specialised approach. Our exterior window vinyl wrap films come with:

  • Advanced UV inhibitors that block yellowing and fading
  • Heat-reflective technology that reduces thermal absorption
  • High-bond adhesives formulated for hot climates
  • Anti-peel edge sealing to maintain longevity

These wraps are used in villas, commercial towers, retail storefronts, hotels, hospitality spaces, and industrial units—delivering a uniform façade transformation that instantly increases visual appeal. Combine them with home and office vinyl wrap UAE solutions for complete upgrades.

The 360Wraps Process – Precision Wrapping From Consultation to Completion

Our methodology blends technical proficiency with design-driven execution:

1. On-Site Analysis & Material Consultation

We assess the structural condition of the frames, environmental exposure, and design goals, then recommend the ideal architectural film (3M, Avery Dennison, KPMF, or equivalent).

2. Pre-Wrap Surface Treatment

Frames are professionally degreased, cleaned, and micro-prepped to ensure adhesive bonding without bubbles, wrinkles, or imperfections.

3. Custom Cutting & Thermal Setting

Using precision cutting tools and controlled heat application, we mould film around corners, bevels, and contours for an OEM-level finish.

4. Seamless Installation & Edge Sealing

Edges are sealed with climate-resistant micro-sealants, ensuring long-term durability against heat, sand, and humidity.

5. Final Quality Inspection

We perform a multi-point visual and tactile inspection, guaranteeing a flawless result every time. Our approach complements services such as furniture vinyl wrap Dubai to maintain consistent quality across all interior elements.
