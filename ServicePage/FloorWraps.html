The Perfect Combination of Style and Practicality


Transform your flooring without demolition, dust, or downtime with 360Wraps’ advanced floor wrapping Dubai solutions. The need for vinyl floor wrapping is growing in both commercial and residential settings, including restaurants, hotels, yachts, offices, elevators, and retail establishments. Vinyl floor wrapping in Dubai provides a quick, long-lasting, and reasonably priced upgrade designed for contemporary interiors, in contrast to traditional flooring replacement, which is expensive and time-consuming.

Why Is Vinyl Floor Wrapping So Well-Liked in Dubai?

Because vinyl floor wrapping combines design, functionality, and long-term performance, it has completely changed interior surface refurbishment. It is favored over traditional flooring because of its:

  • Durability and scratch resistance
  • Moisture resistance for kitchens and bathrooms
  • Heat resistance up to 110°C
  • Minimal maintenance requirements
  • Ability to mimic premium natural materials such as wood, stone, concrete, and marble
  • Fast installation with no downtime

As a result, clients looking for a Dubai floor wrap service choose this solution for fast renovations, rental properties, commercial fit-outs, and luxury upgrades.

Advantages of Vinyl Floor Wrapping in Dubai

1. Affordable Luxury Flooring

Floor wrapping delivers the look of natural wood, marble, stone, or concrete at a fraction of the cost.
Savings range from 50–75% compared to traditional flooring replacement.

2. High-Performance Protection

Architectural-grade vinyl films offer:

  • Waterproof and moisture-blocking layers
  • Heat resistance up to 110°C
  • Anti-scratch and anti-wear coatings
  • Stain-resistant protective surface
  • Antibacterial and easy-to-clean properties

A soft cloth or sponge is all that is needed for maintenance—no special cleaners required.

3. Fast & Hassle-Free Installation

Most vinyl floor wrapping in Dubai projects are completed within 1–2 days, even in large areas.

  • No demolition
  • No noise
  • No debris
  • No interruption to your daily routine

4. Ideal for Any Interior

Floor wraps add instant character, depth, and design expression—reflecting your personal or brand identity. This makes them perfect for:

  • Homes
  • Offices
  • Hospitality spaces
  • Retail stores
  • Yachts
