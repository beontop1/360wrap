Floor Wrapping Dubai: The Perfect Combination of Style and Practicality


Vinyl floor wrapping is in high demand in residential interiors and commercial spaces like offices, restaurants, hotels, even yachts and elevators. This is due to the fact that vinyl films combine durability, moisture resistance, scratch resistance and minimal maintenance costs as opposed to its conventional counterpart option, replacing the flooring that results in long downtime and at a high price. 360 Wraps is perfectly poised in this setor with its advanced floor wrapping services.

Advantages of Vinyl floor wrapping in Dubai

Opting for a Dubai floor wrap service is significantly cheaper than traditional parquet or tile laying. At the same time, floor vinyl wrap gives the same effect of a luxurious floor with the imitation of natural textures like wood, stone, marble, or concrete.

By choosing architectural films, the client receives a protective layer against moisture and stains. Thanks to heat resistant technology, the films can withstand temperatures up to 110 °C. The coating reliably protects the surface from wear, bumps and dirt, and the modern composition makes the material antibacterial and easy to care for. Cleaning the surface is effortless with a soft cloth or sponge. The flooring will not incur damage as long as abrasive materials are not used. Many property owners prefer using floor wrapping Dubai for fast renovations that save time without compromising durability.

All in all, floor wrapping Dubai is the ideal economical option to lend your flooring and overall room a refreshed look while introducing character and depth, reflecting the client's personality.
