OFFICE WRAPS : TRANSFORM YOUR WORKSPACE WITH INNOVATIVE DECOR SOLUTIONS

360 Wraps – Office Wraps


The Power of Office Wraps :

Office wraps encompass a wide range of decorative elements that can breathe new life into any workplace. Here’s why they matter:

  • Visual Impact : Office wraps are designed to make a visual impact. Whether it’s covering walls with inspiring graphics, branding elements, or adding vibrant colors and textures, wraps create a visually engaging atmosphere that reflects your company’s identity.
  • Branding and Identity : Your workspace is a canvas for showcasing your brand and corporate identity. Customized office wraps can incorporate your logo, mission statement, and brand colors, reinforcing your image and values to both employees and visitors.
  • Employee Morale:A well-designed and visually appealing office can boost employee morale and productivity. Office wraps can create a positive and motivating atmosphere, making employees feel more engaged and inspired in their daily tasks.
  • Space Utilization: Office wraps can help you make the most of your space. From creative room dividers to floor graphics that guide foot traffic, these wraps offer functional solutions that enhance the overall layout and organization of your office.

Types of Office Wraps :


  1. Wall Wraps: Transform dull walls into captivating storytelling elements with graphics, murals, or inspirational quotes that reflect your company’s culture and values.
  2. Window Wraps :Enhance privacy, promote branding, or add decorative flair to windows with frosted or perforated window wraps.
  3. Floor Wraps :Guide visitors and employees with floor graphics that can include directional signs, logos, or eye-catching designs.
  4. Furniture Wraps: Revitalize office furniture such as desks, cabinets, or conference tables with wraps that match your decor theme.
  5. Room Dividers: Create separate workspaces or meeting areas with custom room dividers featuring your company’s branding and style.
  6. Meeting Room Wraps: Make your meeting rooms more inviting and functional with wraps that include whiteboard surfaces, acoustic panels, or interactive displays, enhancing collaboration and productivity.
  7. Reception Desk Wraps :The reception area is often the first point of contact for visitors. Wraps on the reception desk can leave a memorable first impression by incorporating your logo and brand messaging.
  8. Kitchen and Breakroom Wraps :Foster a more relaxing and pleasant atmosphere in your kitchen and breakroom areas with wraps that showcase artwork, inspirational quotes, or company achievements.
  9. Hallway Graphics: Utilize the often-overlooked hallway spaces by adding graphics that tell your company’s story, highlight milestones, or feature employee achievements, creating a more engaging work environment.
  10. Custom Wall Decals: These decals can be used to create dynamic and changeable art installations that reflect the evolving nature of your business.
  11. Glass Partition Wraps :Make use of glass partitions between workstations with frosted or branded wraps that provide privacy while maintaining an open and collaborative feel.
  12. Employee Workstation Wraps: Encourage creativity and individuality by allowing employees to personalize their workstations with removable wraps that feature their favorite designs, quotes, or artwork.
  13. Inspirational Quote Wraps: Boost motivation and positivity in your workspace by incorporating motivational quotes or company values into your decor, fostering a more inspiring atmosphere.
  14. Seasonal Decor: Change the ambiance of your office with the seasons. Office wraps can be easily swapped out to celebrate holidays or events, keeping the environment fresh and engaging throughout the year.
  15. Acoustic Wall Wraps :Improve the acoustics in your workspace with specialized wraps designed to absorb sound. These not only create a quieter and more focused atmosphere but also add a stylish touch to your decor.
  16. Eco-Friendly Wraps :Showcase your commitment to sustainability by using eco-friendly materials for your office wraps. These wraps are not only visually appealing but also align with your company’s green initiatives.
  17. Interactive Wall Wraps: Take engagement to the next level with interactive wraps that incorporate touch-sensitive technology or QR codes, allowing employees and visitors to access additional information or participate in activities.
  18. Branded Window Graphics: Extend your branding to the exterior of your office by using window wraps that display your logo, tagline, or promotional messages, making a strong impression on passersby.
  19. Event Promotion :Utilize wraps to promote upcoming events or important announcements within your office space, keeping everyone informed and engaged.
  20. Employee Recognition Walls :Dedicate a space in your office to recognize employee achievements and milestones with custom wraps that celebrate their contributions.
  21. Health and Safety Wraps: In response to health concerns, use wraps to communicate important safety information, such as social distancing guidelines or handwashing instructions.
  22. Custom Artwork: Commission or create custom artwork for your office wraps that reflects your company’s culture, values, and history, turning your workspace into a gallery of inspiration.
  23. Collaborative Workspaces: Design collaborative spaces with wraps that feature brainstorming surfaces, fostering creativity and idea-sharing among teams.
