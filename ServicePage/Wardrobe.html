What is a Wardrobe Wrap?

A wardrobe wrapping service Dubai is the application of interior architectural film on doors, frames, and matching wardrobe parts. The self-adhesive base ensures quick installation without building dust. A variety of finishes and textures, such as wood, marble, stone, and metal, are available. The coating is easy to clean, resistant to standard care products and designed for intensive indoor use.

FAQs

Every project is carried out on a turnkey basis. This includes detailed measurement, presentation of samples, preparation of the base, careful cutting and edges, and quality control with wardrobe wrapping before and after photos.

Every project depends on the requirements. For a standard cabinet, work usually takes 1 day. Complex projects take up to 1-3 days. Fittings and, if necessary, doors are removed, but the body is not dismantled. Noise and dust are minimal due to precise preparation and clean installation.

The film bonds reliably to MDF and particleboard, painted plastics, metal, and glass. Preparation includes a site measurement, thorough cleaning and degreasing, correction of minor defects with light sanding, dust removal, and a small-area adhesion test before full application to confirm a secure bond for your wardrobe wrap.
