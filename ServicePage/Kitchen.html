What is Kitchen Wrapping?

It is a modern interior renovation technology using durable self-adhesive vinyl film. We cover cabinets, countertops and walls with it, instantly updating the kitchen without noise and dust. Compared to expensive fixes, this restoration is more affordable.

The wrap material has a resistance to heat up to 110°C, moisture, and scratches, which makes it best suited for the UAE's climate. With a lot of options to choose from, a variety of matte, glossy, wood, marble textures and 3D finishes, the options are not few.

Advantages of Kitchen Wrapping

  • An Economical Solution : kitchen wrap dubai allows you to update the interior without incurring capital expenses, giving you the effect of a new space at a minimal cost.
  • Quick Installation : We offer quick installation without construction work and downtime. Your kitchen completely changes in a few days.
  • Resistance to Heat and Moisture : The heat-resistant vinyl used can withstand temperatures up to 110°C, and moisture resistance prevents swelling and delamination of surfaces.
  • Durability and Reliability: The film is resistant to scratches, stains and humidity, and the scratch-resistant coating retains its perfect appearance for many years to come.
  • Environmental Friendliness : A sustainable renovation that uses eco-friendly materials and minimizes construction debris.
  • Design Flexibility : Choose from dozens of architectural finishes: wood, marble, matte and glossy shades, and 3D textured wrap to create a unique design.
  • Ease of Care : The surfaces are easy to clean, just wipe them with a soft cloth, and the kitchen looks like new again.
