What is Elevator Wrapping?

Elevator wrapping is a modern finishing technology in which a long-lasting self-adhesive vinyl film is put directly to the elevator's surface, including doors, walls, and internal panels. Instead of painting or completely replacing materials, a flexible coating is used that instantly renews the appearance. The process takes little time, is clean and neat, and can be disassembled simply if necessary. Because of the many textures - matte, glossy, metallic, wood, or marble, the décor may be tailored to any architectural design. This strategy saves money and time while producing high-quality, long-term outcomes.


Advantages of Elevator Wrapping

  • Budget Savings : Elevator vinyl wrap is significantly cheaper than a complete cladding replacement or paint job, while the upgrade effect is identical.
  • Durability and Reliability : The materials we use are scratch-resistant, moisture-resistant, and temperature-resistant, so your elevator retains its impeccable appearance for many years.
  • Design Flexibility : You can choose any design option: from minimalistic matte surfaces to luxurious metal and wooden textures. Our interior elevator wraps will help to emphasize the style of your space.
  • Quick Installation : Installation takes only a few hours, without dust, noise and downtime. The work is carried out carefully and without interfering with the operation of the building.
  • Ease of Care : The vinyl coating is easy to clean, just wipe with a soft cloth to restore shine and freshness.
  • Environmental Friendliness : The film extends the service life of existing materials, reduces construction waste, and helps make repairs more sustainable.
