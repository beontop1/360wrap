How 360Wraps Installs Your Kitchen Countertop Wrap

Our installation process is designed for maximum precision and seamless adhesion:

  1. Surface Assessment: We inspect the worktop for cracks, bumps, or moisture issues.
  2. Deep Cleaning & De-greasing: Using industrial-grade cleaners for optimal adhesion.
  3. Surface Preparation: Light sanding if required, edge correction & repair work.
  4. Film Application: Heat activation technique ensures tight wrapping around edges, corners, and profiles.
  5. Finishing & Sealing: Bubble-free smoothing, edge sealing, and final quality check.

This meticulous process ensures your new worktop looks professionally refurbished not wrapped.

Maintenance of Vinyl-Wrapped Countertops

To maintain long-lasting beauty:

  • Use chopping boards (avoid direct cutting on vinyl)
  • Use heat pads for very hot pots
  • Wipe spills promptly
  • Avoid abrasive cleaners or metal scrubbers
  • Clean with mild soap & microfiber cloth

This keeps your vinyl worktop looking fresh for years.
