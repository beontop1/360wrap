What is Countertop Wrapping?

Countertop wrapping is a modern trend of kitchen wrapping Dubai, which uses self-adhesive vinyl or architectural film. The film is glued directly onto the existing surface, instantly giving it a new look. The material is durable and safe: it is heat-resistant up to 110 °C, moisture-resistant, scratch-resistant, and stain-resistant, making it easy to withstand kitchen use.

Dozens of designs are available, matte, glossy, marble vinyl wrap, wood grain, granite effect, everything looks realistic and natural. This method is suitable for those who want a cost-effective renovation without spending time and money on furniture replacement, and is ideally combined with the services of kitchen cabinet wrap dubai and kitchen vinyl wrap Dubai.
