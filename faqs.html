General information

Interior wrapping is a quick and easy procedure that modifies the texture or look of existing surfaces by putting self-adhesive vinyl to them.

Almost any surface in any location, including wood, metal, glass, plastic, and other painted surfaces, can be covered with vinyl wraps.

Nothing needs to be taken apart or disassembled because everything is covered in vinyl while it is installed.

Vinyl is extremely reliable and impact-resistant. It can be cleaned with mild detergents without causing harm due to its resistant to moisture and bacteria.

We advise washing with a microfiber or soft cloth and mild soapy detergents or disinfectants for longer durability.
